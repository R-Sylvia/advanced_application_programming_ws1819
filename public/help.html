How to Grab IT
Manual
Grab I T is a simple “pick and run” multiplayer game.
You have to register yourselfe first before beeing allowed to join the game.
After a successful registration and login you can eigher start the game or have a look on the scoreboard with all registered players and their highest scores.
You can navigate your avatar through the world by using the W, A, S and D keys of your keyboard.
For moving your avatar forward, use "W".
For moving your avatar backward, use "S".
For moving your avatar left, use "A".
For moving your avatar right, use "D".
The goal of Grab I T is to collect as many as possible geometric objects which are placed in the world to move up the highscore ladder.
For collecting the geometric figures, your avatar needs to touch them.
Afterwards the geometrical object will dissapear and your score will increase.
You can find your highest score in the separated scorebaord.
While playing, you can virtually meet your counterparts and chat with them.
